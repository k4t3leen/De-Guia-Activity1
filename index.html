How To Make: Crispy Fried Chicken

Perfectly crunchy and tasty for every meal.

author: Kateleen De Guia

If you've wanted to learn how to make crispy fried chicken, this is the recipe for you.


What is the secret for a crunchy chicken?

Typically, chicken should be fried for about 7-8 minutes per side. But keep in mind that pieces can vary in size and thickness.

The chicken is fully cooked when the thickest part of the meat registers at 165° and the skin is crispy and dark golden-brown in color.

Let's Get Started!

Ingredients List

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour, divided
  • 2 tablespoons garlic salt
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 3 teaspoons pepper, divided
  • 2-1/2 teaspoons poultry seasoning
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1-1/2 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 broiler/fryer chickens (3-1/2 to 4 pounds each), cut up
  • Oil for deep-fat frying

Step 1

In a large shallow dish, combine flour, garlic salt, paprika, pepper and poultry seasoning.

Step 2

In a deep-fat fryer, heat oil to 375°

Step 3

Fry chicken, several pieces at a time, until chicken is golden brown and juices run clear

Step 4

Drain on heating rack. let cool down

Last step!

Top it off with some herbs and serve with special sauce.

And you're done!

Perfect meal everytime.
